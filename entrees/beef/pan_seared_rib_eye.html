Pan-Seared Rib-Eye

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount Note
Boneless rib eye steak 1-1/2 inches thick 1
Canola oil To coat the steak
Kosher salt To taste

Instructions

Preparation

  1. Bring the steak to room temperature.
  2. Place a 10-to-12 inch skillet in the oven and heat the oven to 500F.

Cooking

Note: Cooking times given are for a medium-rare steak. For medium, add 1 minute to both of the oven turns.

  1. When the oven reaches temperature, remove the skillet.
  2. Place the skillet on the range over high heat for 5 minutes.
  3. Coat the steak lightly with oil.
  4. Sprinkle both sides with a generous pinch of salt.
  5. Grind on black pepper.
  6. Immediately place the steak in the middle of the hot, dry skillet.
  7. Cook 30 seconds without moving.
  8. Turn with tongs and cook another 30 seconds.
  9. Put the pan straight into the oven for 2 minutes.
  10. Flip the steak and cook for another 2 minutes.
  11. Remove the steak from the skillet.
  12. Loosely cover the steak with foil and let it rest for 2 minutes.
  13. Serve.
