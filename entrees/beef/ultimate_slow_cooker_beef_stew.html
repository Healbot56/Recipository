Ultimate Slow Cooker Beef Stew

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount Note
Chuck Roast 3 lbs
Kosher salt 1 tsp
Coarse ground black pepper 1/2 tsp
Yellow onion, chopped 1
Carrots 4 Cut into 2-inch chunks
Yukon potatoes 4 Chopped
Beef broth 4 cups
Bay leaf 1
Thyme 1 tsp
Garlic cloves 4 Minced
Worcestershire sauce 1 tbsp
Unsalted butter 2 tbsp
Flour 2 tbsp

Instructions

  1. Preheat an aluminum slow cooker insert or cast iron skillet on high heat.
  2. Season the beef with half the salt and all of the pepper.
  3. Add the beef to the skillet and brown on each side for 4-6 minutes or until deeply browned on each side.
  4. Remove the beef from the pan and add in the onions.
  5. Cook the onions for 3 minutes, stir, then cook for another 3 minutes.
  6. Into the slow cooker, add the:
    1. Onions
    2. carrots
    3. Potatoes
    4. Broth
    5. The second half of the salt
    6. Tomato paste
    7. Bay leaf
    8. Thyme
    9. Garlic
    10. Worcestershire sauce.
  7. Cut the beef into 2-3 inch chunks and add it to the slow cooker.
  8. Stir all the ingredients until well mixed
  9. Cook on low for 7 hours.
  10. In a small bowl, mix the butter and flour and add it to the slow cooker and stir.
  11. Cook an additional hour or until thickened.
